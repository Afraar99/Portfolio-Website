    About Me

    I’m Mohamed Afraar, a third-year Computing & Information Systems undergraduate at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, currently interning as a Software Engineer at SLT Digital Labs. With over two years of experience in ReactJS, JavaScript, and UI/UX design, I’m passionate about creating impactful and user-friendly software solutions.
